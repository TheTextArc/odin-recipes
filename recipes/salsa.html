Salsa

Description:

Salsa made with fresh vegetables to dip your chips in.

Adapted from Leanne Brown's "Good and Cheap"

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 medium onion, finely diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, finely diced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/4 fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper

Optional additions

  • mango, peach, plu, or pineapple
  • Beans
  • Corn
  • Garlic

Instructions

  1. If you don't want raw onions, saute the onions in a pan with a bit of water over medium heat until the water has boiled off.
  2. You can substitute cilantro with another herb if you want to.
  3. Mix the ingredients into a bowl. Add salt and pepper as needed.
  4. Taste the salsa to your liking. The salsa consists of spiciness from the peppers, sweetness from the tomatoes, and freshness from the herbs and lime.
  5. Add the appropriate ingridients as needed if you want more of a certain flavour.
  6. When storing, make sure the container is air tight. Make sure to eat as soon as possible since it won't last as long as store bought salsa.
